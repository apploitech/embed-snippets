Discover An Opportunity

Fashion. Passion. Vision. The fast pace is thrilling. The challenges come quickly and the opportunities are limitless. We hire world-class talent with intelligence and a drive for success. We pride ourselves on our collaborative work environment that celebrates diversity in people and ideas. We are looking for associates who show initiative, are great team players, and who achieve results. Sound like you? Take a look at one of our many opportunities in one of our stores across the country or at our Brand Headquarters.

Brand Headquarters And Store Management Positions

Located in midtown Manhattan with convenient commuter access, Brand Headquarters houses most of the company’s major functions: Merchandising, Design, Marketing, E-Commerce, Finance, Human Resources, IT, Loss Prevention, Purchasing and Facilities, Production and Sourcing, Planning and Allocation, Real Estate and Store Operations. Click Here to view our job openings at Brand Headquarters and for management job openings at our stores.
