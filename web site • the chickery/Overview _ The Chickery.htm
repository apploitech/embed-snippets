    PREMIUM QUALITY, REAL TASTY CHICKEN AND SIDES

    The Chickery menu will change your expectations of what fast food can be! Founded in 2012, in Toronto, Canada – The Chickery is growing worldwide. The Chickery is more than real tasty chicken and sides! The Chickery is a pioneer in Better Chicken. We’ve built a community that is passionate about food: how it tastes, where it came from, how it is prepared, and how it makes you feel.  Quite simply, we strive to do everything better than our competitors.

    What is Better Chicken?

    Better Ingredients: Fresh. All-Natural. Never Frozen. Real ingredients you can pronounce.

    Better Tasting: Flavorful, handcrafted chef-driven recipes.

    Better Feeling: Our menu is made by hand, by real people, that serve you with a smile. Our restaurants have soul and good music. We like to laugh, and we love good food.

    Better Quality: Our menu is full of mouthwatering, premium dishes you simply won’t find at other quick serve chicken restaurants.

    Better Value: Gourmet quality, premium chicken at fast casual prices.

    OUR RESTAURANTS

    We’re a little bit soul and a little rock ‘n’ roll in a comfy, modern and warm atmosphere. You’ll meet a casual crowd gathering to celebrate good food, friends, and a menu full of real tasty choices.

    INGREDIENTS

    Ingredients Matter. Your potato salad should be made from real potatoes and your mac & cheese from real cheese. Everything comes to the store as a whole ingredient. Our chicken is all-natural, fresh and never frozen. Any ingredient used is something you can pronounce.
